Welcome To Alice Archery Club

ALICE ARCHERY CLUB (AAC) is an private and non-for-profit archery information club located at Wilmington Delaware. All information is free and open to those interested in all aspects of archery including field archery and 3-D archery with compound bows or traditional bows. Crossbows are currently not included.

  • Archery is the practice or skill of using a bow to shoot arrows.
  • The word comes from the Latin arcus meaning ‘bow’ or ‘arch’.
  • Historically, archery has been used for hunting and combat and was invented in the late Palaeolithic or early Mesolithic periods.
  • Archery is often in the public eye, both as an important sport on the Olympic Programme, and thanks to popular culture.
  • Archery is addictive. You have been warned!
